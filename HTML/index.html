Mission Statement:

 

At Cougar Courier, we believe in the power of community. As your University of Houston Post Office, we are committed to weaving the fabric of our neighborhood tighter, one letter, one parcel, one smile at a time. Guided by the spirit of Uma, our Puma mascot, we pledge to provide prompt, reliable, and personal postal services, ensuring that every piece of mail bridges hearts and nurtures connections.
